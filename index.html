Common questions

Straight answers.

Is my wage information private?

Always. Submissions are anonymized before they enter the benchmark. We never display individual wages — only aggregated percentiles. Your employer cannot see what you submitted.

How do you know wages are real?

We seed from public and FOIA-able payroll data, then verify worker submissions using paystub uploads, email-domain matching, and statistical outlier detection. Junk gets filtered out.

Why "give to get"?

Because pay data only stays current if real workers keep contributing. One submission unlocks full access; the benchmark gets sharper for every nurse who comes after you.

Can my employer pay you to change my read?

No. Employers can earn certification by paying fairly, and pay for recruiting access — but they have zero ability to move the benchmark, suppress data, or alter percentiles. That wall is the product.
